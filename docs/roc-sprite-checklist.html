🪨 The Spout Roc Dojo — Sprite Checklist

To replace any Roc's placeholder art with your own: export a transparent, square PNG (256×256 or larger), name it exactly the filename shown, and drop it in src/assets/rocs/. No code changes — it swaps in automatically and inherits the idle/cheer animations. You can do them one at a time. The little vector below each name is the current placeholder, just for reference — match the vibe, not the shapes.

Suggested style: a chibi training rock character, bold outline, readable at 32px (it appears small on the leaderboard). Keep a consistent baseline so they line up. The belt color is drawn by the app on top in some places, but bake a belt into the art too for the hero view.
