# 🚀 Getting Started — The Spout Roc Dojo

**Open it:** https://dojoroc-dojoroc.vercel.app

Works on a phone, tablet, or computer. No download required (but you can install it).

---

## First 5 minutes

1. **Create your profile.** Pick a display name, a color, and a password.
   👉 **Save the recovery code it shows you** — it's how you get back in if you forget
   your password. (No email needed.)

2. **Meet your Roc.** You start with a companion Roc. Open the **Rocs** tab to see it,
   rename it, and tap it to make it wobble. This little character is *yours* — it levels
   up and travels everywhere with you.

3. **Do the daily quests.** On the **Missions** tab you'll see 3 quick goals for today.
   Knock them out for XP. Come back tomorrow to keep your **streak** — it multiplies your
   rewards (up to 2×).

4. **Talk to your sensei.** Open the **[Sensei] AI** tab and ask anything. Try the
   one-tap buttons like **Quiz Me** or **Explain Simply**. Hit **Remember this** on a reply
   to teach your Roc something about you.

5. **Explore the gyms.** Tap **Rooms** to see the **Dojo Map** — walk your Roc into a new
   territory: Space, Math, Art, or Music. Each has its own game (trace constellations,
   math sprint, draw, or make beats).

---

## How to grow

- **Earn belts** by clearing missions, winning sparring matches (Rocs tab), and playing.
- **Unlock more** as your belt climbs: new personalities, abilities, wardrobe items, and
  rarer Roc species to recruit.
- **Spar** to train your Roc fast — pick Easy / Even / Hard in the Rocs tab.
- **Dress up your Roc** — equip wardrobe in the Rocs tab, or set one of your own saved
  drawings as its badge.
- **Gift a Roc** to a friend by their username (Rocs tab → Gift button).

## Show it off
- **Share your portfolio:** Profile tab → **Share portfolio**. Anyone can view your belt,
  XP, and signature Roc at `dojoroc-dojoroc.vercel.app/?u=yourusername`.
- **Install it:** in your phone browser, tap Share → **Add to Home Screen**.

## If you get stuck
- Forgot your password? On the login screen tap **Forgot your password?** and use your
  recovery code.
- Lost the recovery code too? Whoever set this up can reset it from the database.

That's it — pick a gym, train your Roc, and have fun. 🪨
