# 🪨 The Spout Roc Dojo

**A learning game where you train your own AI companion.**

👉 **Live app:** https://dojoroc-dojoroc.vercel.app

Kids (and grown-ups) raise collectible **Roc** characters by exploring **gyms** —
subjects like space, math, art, music, and coding. Each Roc is your own AI buddy: it
remembers you, levels up martial-arts belts, evolves, unlocks personalities, abilities,
and outfits, reacts with personality, and travels with you across every gym and device.
